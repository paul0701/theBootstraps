The Bootstraps Story

The Bootstraps formed in 2011 after producers Marc Otto & Jacob Thornton had the idea to form a super group. Hailing from Manchester, England the three members, Julian, Ruben & Paulo are all avid Manchester United fans.Lead singer Julian wrote the bands first hit "Front-end toolkit" in late 2011. The group went on to complete a world-tour in 2012, writing another hit single "Breadcrumb" the same year. The Bootstraps sound is so unique, and unlike anything else, it is like they went back in time and picked the best music from history and re-wrote it as their own. This theory became viral on social media and was even featured in an episode the hit TV show Dr Who.

Since their inception The Bootstraps have had global success including:

  • 11 Grammys
  • 21 MTV Video Music Awards
  • 4 NME Awards
  • 6 Platinum Albums
  • 14 Number One hits
